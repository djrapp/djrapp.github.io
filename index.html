About Me About

I’m an innovator, a deep generalist, and a believer in creating things which meaningfully touch and improve people’s lives. Currently, I'm a Product Manager at Whisper.ai, where we are creating hearing aids that use AI to improve over time. Previously, I drove development of cutting-edge hardware products (like the Surface Hub 2 Smart Camera) as a Senior Product Manager with the Applied Sciences incubation team at Microsoft.

I studied Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Colorado, followed by a master's in Human Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington. I love to draw on both these backgrounds in my work, tackling complex, real-world problems and building creative solutions.

In my spare time, you'll find me doting on my amazing cat Rocco, turning knobs on synthesizers, and probably cooking away on a side project or two. Want to make something cool together? Get in touch.
